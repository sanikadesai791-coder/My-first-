Farmer Consumer

Farmer Consumer is a community-based project developed as part of the Community Engagement Project (CEP). The website is designed and developed using HTML and CSS.

A Farmer Consumer website is an online platform that directly connects farmers with consumers. Farmers can sell fresh agricultural products like vegetables, fruits, grains, and dairy without middlemen. This helps farmers get a fair price, and consumers receive fresh, quality food at lower cost. The website usually includes features such as product listing, online ordering, secure payment, and home delivery.
